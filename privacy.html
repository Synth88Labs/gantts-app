Privacy Policy

Short version: gantts.app has no accounts and no server-side storage of your projects. Your Gantt charts live in your own browser. Last updated July 15, 2026.

Your project data

When you create a Gantt chart, it is saved in your browser's local storage on your device. It is not transmitted to us or stored on our servers. If you clear your browser data, your projects are removed. You are in full control: you can export a JSON backup, a CSV, or a shareable link at any time.

Shareable links

When you generate a shareable link, your project data is encoded directly into the link (the part after #). This means the data travels inside the URL and is not stored on a server. Only people you send the link to can open it.

What we don't collect

  • We don't require an email, name, or account.
  • We don't upload or read the contents of your Gantt charts.
  • We don't sell data — we don't have your data to sell.

Analytics & hosting

Like most websites, our web host may keep standard server logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and performance. If we add privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics to understand which pages are useful, we will use aggregate data only. We will update this page if that changes.

Third-party libraries

The app loads a few open-source libraries (for exporting to PDF, Excel and PowerPoint) from a public CDN. These requests are made by your browser to fetch code; they don't include your project data.

Cookies

gantts.app does not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's local storage only to save your projects and preferences (like light/dark theme) on your own device.

Contact

Questions about privacy? This is a small, independent project — reach out via the site and we'll do our best to help.

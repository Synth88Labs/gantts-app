What's included

The template walks through every stage of an event, with milestones on the deadlines you can't miss:

  • Concept & budget — goals, theme, budget, date and initial guest list. Milestone: budget approved.
  • Venue & vendors — venue booking, catering, AV, décor, and contracts. Milestone: venue confirmed.
  • Marketing & registration — invitations, website / ticketing, social promotion and reminders. Milestone: invitations sent.
  • Logistics — run-of-show, staffing and volunteers, signage, transport and rehearsals. Milestone: final headcount.
  • Event day — setup, registration desk, the program itself, and teardown.
  • Post-event — thank-yous, vendor payments, feedback survey and a wrap-up report.
The date is fixed — plan backwards. Set event day as a milestone in the free gantts.app editor and link deadlines to it. "Confirm final numbers" and "send reminders" then land on exactly the right day, and the critical path shows what truly can't slip. No sign up.

How to customize it

  1. Set your event date first — it's the anchor everything counts back from.
  2. Swap in your event type. Keep the tasks that fit a wedding, conference, webinar or fundraiser and drop the rest.
  3. Add your vendors as tasks with owners and confirm-by dates.
  4. Link the dependencies — you can't send invitations before the venue is confirmed, or finalize catering before headcount.
  5. Mark the hard deadlines (deposits, RSVPs, final numbers) as milestones.

Event scheduling tips

  • Book the long-lead items first. Popular venues and vendors get reserved months out — put those at the front and treat them as blocking.
  • Leave buffer before the date. Keep the final week light so you can absorb surprises instead of scrambling.
  • Track deposit and cancellation deadlines as milestones — missing a deposit date can lose a venue.
  • Build a run-of-show for the day itself with tasks in hours rather than days.
  • Share with vendors. Export to PDF or PowerPoint so caterers, AV and venue staff all work from the same timeline.

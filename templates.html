FAQ

Template questions, answered

Are these Gantt chart templates really free?

Yes. Every template is 100% free to download and free to edit online — no account, no trial, and no credit card. You can export your finished chart to PDF, PNG, Excel and PowerPoint at no cost.

Do I need to sign up to download a template?

No. There is no sign up and no login. Click a download button to grab the file, or click "Edit online free" to open the template directly in your browser.

What file formats can I download?

Most templates are available as Excel (.xlsx), PowerPoint (.pptx) and CSV. You can also open any template in the online editor and export to PDF or PNG as well.

Can I use these templates in Google Sheets?

Yes. Download the Excel or CSV file and use File → Import in Google Sheets, or start from the Google Sheets template. You can also skip Sheets entirely and edit online.

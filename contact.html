Report a bug or request a feature

The fastest route for anything technical is a GitHub issue — it's public, so other people hit by the same problem can find it and add detail.

  • Open an issue — bugs and feature requests
  • Browse the source — the whole app is open source under the MIT licence

What helps when reporting a bug

Because everything runs in your browser and nothing is uploaded, we can't look up your project to investigate. That means the details matter:

  • Which browser and operating system you're on
  • What you did, what you expected, and what happened instead
  • If you can, a saved .gantts file that reproduces it — attach it to the email or issue

A note on your data

We have no access to your charts. Nothing you build is stored on a server, so we can't recover a lost project, and we'll never ask you to send credentials or personal details. See the privacy policy for the full picture.

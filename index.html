New to Gantt charts? Start here →
Is gantts.app really free?

Yes — 100% free with no paid tiers, no trial, and no credit card. Every feature, including export and critical path, is free forever.

Do I need to sign up or log in?

No. There's no account, no sign up, and no login. Open the site and start building your Gantt chart immediately in your browser.

Do I need to download or install anything?

No. It runs entirely in your web browser on any device — nothing to install. You can even add it to your home screen and use it offline.

Can I export to Excel or PowerPoint?

Yes. Export to PDF, PNG, Excel (.xlsx), and PowerPoint (.pptx) for free, so you can share, print, or drop it straight into a presentation.

Does it show the critical path?

Yes. gantts.app automatically calculates and highlights the critical path — the sequence of tasks that determines your project's finish date. Learn how it works →

How is my work saved?

It autosaves in your browser as you go. You can also save a .gantts file to your computer and re-open it any time, or copy a shareable link.

Can I use it on my phone or tablet?

Yes — it works in any modern browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile, and installs to your home screen.
